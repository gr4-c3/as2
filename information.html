Information

Here is some information about me!

As aforementioned I am a student at the University of Aberdeen, currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics. I have a passion for technology amongst other things, I specialise in aerospace technology predominantly and am on the committee for the AESOC at our own university. In my free time I enjoy playing video games on a professional level, I am a top 30 world player in World of Warcraft which has earned me both recognition and opportunities that I would not be able to have otherwise. I also am a keen ice-dancer, I used to skate on a professional level when I was younger, now I skate from time to time more casually.

Ice Skating Career

I used to compete for the british solo ice dancing team internationally from the age of 7. At the age of 11 I had placed 3rd in the british national championships for my category while being 2 years younger than my peers. I spent 6 days a week training, predominantly waking up at 5am every morning to practice before school. Not only that but I had to juggle a variety of sports on top to increase my flexibility and coordination as well as be able to participate in school sports such as netball and rounders. Overall I was a dedicated athlete and I went to the British Championships multiple years in a row. Often we had international competitions in Helsinki, Copenhagen and further. Although by no means was it official, I did skate in a speed competition in the far reaches of New Zealand one year.

Gaming Career

From a young age, I have been passionate about video games and their competitive aspects. It started when I was about 4 years old playing minecraft with my dad in the living room after school and as I grew older my passion for competitive gaming only increased. By 15 I was competing in the Valorant Womens League (Game Changers EU) and since I was 18 I have started playing World of Warcraft which I have quickly progressed to becoming a top 30 player worldwide. I enjoy the game as it encourages teambuilding and strategising and no one boss fight will play out the same as all mechanics are RNG and require skill as you progress further.

Aeronautics

I have always been fascinated by vehicles of all sorts, from trains as a baby to aircraft as I grew older. This interest led me to explore the field of aerospace engineering and design, where I have since developed a deep appreciation for the science and technology behind flight. WIth confidence I will pick out planes in the sky and tell you about them, I have attended many air shows and tattoos and got to seen the wonders of planes such as the B-52 Stratofortress and the Harrier Jumpjet. I love the infrastructure of aircraft and how the flight surfaces communicate with each other through a thousand different sensors. Personally I think aircraft are absolute marvels of technology and that supersonic jet travel will make its way back into existence again. Had I been old enough to experience the golden age of the concorde I would have been delighted. It's a shame too that the jumbo jet era is over but alas perhaps it is not such a bad thing as aircraft move towards being more green and fuel efficient for our environment.
